About Me

How you doin

Hello! I'm Dorothy, a medical student who consumes far too much coffee. I decided to make this blog in 2017 (and then didn't bother to start it until 2020) after I realized I go to far too many cafes to not actually document the experience. I also have attempted to rate and review those experiences, but hey, I'm just one person so it's always better to try a place out for yourself than to take my word for it.

The name "Kafeinated" comes from the Chinese characters for coffee (咖啡, romanized as Kāfēi). Credit to Jamie Sison for coming up with the name, as I am far too uncreative to have thought of it.

If you have a cafe suggestion for me to (attempt to) try, or want to talk about coffee in general than hit me up!
